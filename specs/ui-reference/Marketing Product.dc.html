HOW IT WORKS

A governed pipeline, not a chatbot

Every question runs through nine deterministic stages. The LLM plans; validated SQL and gates do the work. Click a stage to jump to it.

1Understand 2Validate metrics 3Build gold data 4Train & backtest 5Assemble & share
STAGE 1 · UNDERSTAND

Your question becomes a reviewable plan

AnalytIQ resolves your words against the semantic layer — governed metrics, known dimensions, allowed joins. When it isn't sure, it asks one crisp question instead of guessing. You approve the plan before anything runs.

STAGE 2 · VALIDATE

Deterministic gates, not vibes

Every metric resolves to one reviewed definition. Dangerous joins are blocked, PII is masked, and generated SQL must pass safety checks before it can touch your warehouse — read-only, always.

STAGE 3 · BUILD

An immutable gold table per answer

Each dashboard is fed by a versioned, contract-checked gold table at exactly the right grain. Row counts, reconciliation, and null contracts are enforced — if a gate fails, the build repairs or stops. No silent bad data.

STAGE 4 · PREDICT

Forecasts earn their place

When your question needs a prediction, candidates compete on rolling backtests with automatic leakage checks. A champion is promoted only when it beats the incumbent — and ships with a model card anyone can read.

STAGE 5 · SHIP

A living artifact, not a screenshot

The result is a versioned dashboard with health scores, narrative insight, and a full audit trail. Refine it in chat, edit it directly, or share it with signed, expiring links and embeds.
